The Ontario Headwaters Institute works to protect Ontario's watersheds, their natural heritage, and receiving waters such as the Great Lakes, through on-going efforts in our three portfolios on Research, Education, and Civic Engagement. In February, the latter portfolio launched the Coalition of Ontario Water Guardians, with numerous partners, as can be seen at www.cowg.ca. In March, the Coalition launched its first collaborative effort: a petition asking the province to better protect water security. See our blog for more.

About Our Project

We as Fleming GIS program students created this website based on our client's request. This website is part of the outcome of the project "Evaluation Of Watershed Hydrology And Landuse In The Greater Golden Horseshoe". This website is also part of the final deliverables for our APST62 course. The solution webpage contains the web map required by the client and the methodology page describes our steps of implementation. Finally, we would like to take this opportunity to thank every faculty members who provided us with technical support and advice at Fleming College.
